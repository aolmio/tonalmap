💡 Quick Mnemonics

Mid Class: What you see is what you get! The tone marks match their standard names perfectly.
Low Class Shift (+1 Rule): Low class consonants are rebels. An Ek mark (่) shifts the output up to a Falling tone, and a Tho mark (้) shifts it all the way up to a High tone.
The Dead End: If there are no tone marks and the syllable is dead, High/Mid class drop deep down into a Low Tone.
